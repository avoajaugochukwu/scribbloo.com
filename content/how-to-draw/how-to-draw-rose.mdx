subtitle: A simple, step-by-step rose tutorial for all ages.
order: 165000
---

1. Draw a small spiral for the center.
2. Add a teardrop shape around it.
3. Build up the inner petals.
4. Add the outer petals.
5. Draw the stem and leaves.
6. Ink and color your rose.
