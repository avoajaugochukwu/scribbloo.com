1. Sketch the head and snout.
2. Add the long, curving neck.
3. Draw the body and chest.
4. Add the wings.
5. Sketch the legs and claws.
6. Add the tail.
7. Draw the scales and spikes.
8. Ink, erase your guides, and color.
