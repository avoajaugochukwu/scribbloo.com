Looking for summer things to draw? You landed in the sunniest spot online. This list is packed with easy summer drawings built from simple shapes like circles, triangles, and gentle waves. No skill needed. If you can draw a beach ball and a melting ice cream cone, you can make almost everything here. We cover the beach and ocean, frosty summer treats, sunny weather, fun summer activities, and warm weather animals. The secret to that bright summer feeling is simple: round shapes and cheerful colors. Grab a pencil and let's soak up some sunshine on paper.

## How to use this list

Pick any idea that grabs you. You do not have to go in order. Each idea names the easy starting shape so you always know where to begin. Draw light at first, then darken the lines you love. When you are done, color it in with sky blue, sunny yellow, and bright coral for that warm summer glow.

## Beach and ocean

### Beach ball
A big circle split into curved colorful panels. Add a small shine spot for that round, bouncy look.

### Sandcastle
A few stacked rectangles with little triangle flags on top. Add a wavy moat line around the base.

### Seashell
A fan shape with curved lines spreading out from the bottom point. Add a small bump at the base.

### Starfish
A chubby five point star with rounded arms. Dot the top with tiny circles for texture.

### Surfboard
A long rounded oval with a center stripe down the middle. Stand it up in the sand at a slant.

### Palm tree
A curved trunk line with droopy leaf shapes fanning from the top. Add a coconut or two.

### Beach umbrella
A half circle dome with curved ribs underneath and a tall pole. Color it in cheerful stripes.

### Ocean wave
A curling C shape with a foamy edge of small bumps. Add a few swirl lines inside the curl.

### Crab
A wide oval body with two claw shapes and skinny legs poking out the sides. Add two stalk eyes.

### Flip flops
Two simple sole shapes with a Y strap on each. Tilt them like they were just kicked off.

## Cold summer treats

### Ice cream cone
A triangle cone with round scoops stacked on top. Add drips and a cherry for the win.

### Popsicle
A rounded rectangle on a thin stick. Add a few drip lines and color it bright.

### Watermelon slice
A half circle with a green rind edge and little seed dots inside. So juicy and easy.

### Lemonade glass
A tall cup shape with a straw and a lemon slice on the rim. Add bubbles and a few ice cubes.

### Snow cone
A paper cone shape with a rounded mound of ice on top. Color it in rainbow layers.

### Ice cream sundae
A round bowl with scoops, a swirl of cream, and a cherry. Add sprinkle dots for fun.

### Fruit smoothie
A wide cup with a dome lid and a straw. Color it pink or purple and add a little fruit on top.

### Slice of pie
A triangle wedge with a wavy crust edge. Add a scoop of round vanilla on the side.

## Sunny weather

### Smiling sun
A circle with triangle rays around it and a happy face in the middle. The classic summer doodle.

### Fluffy cloud
A bumpy row of half circles in a long shape. Add a tiny sun peeking out behind it.

### Rainbow
A set of stacked arch lines from wide to small. Pop a fluffy cloud at each end.

### Sunglasses
Two rounded lens shapes joined by a small bridge. Add little arms on the sides.

### Sun hat
A wide brim oval with a rounded dome on top. Tie a ribbon band around the base.

### Sunscreen bottle
A tall rounded bottle with a flip cap. Add a sun logo and a label on the front.

### Kite
A diamond shape with a long tail of little bows. Add a string trailing down to a hand.

### Beach towel
A rectangle with stripe lines or a fun pattern. Add a little fringe edge at the ends.

## Summer activities

### Bicycle
Two circles for wheels joined by a frame of straight lines. Add a seat, handlebars, and a basket.

### Picnic basket
A woven basket shape with a curved handle and a checkered cloth peeking out the top.

### Camping tent
A triangle with a flap door and a little zipper line. Add a tiny flag on the peak.

### Fishing pole
A long thin line with a small reel circle. Add a wavy fishing line and a tiny hook.

### Sandals and a bucket
A simple bucket shape with a handle and a small shovel leaning against it. Beach day ready.

### Pool float
A wide ring shape, like a donut, or a duck float with a round head. Add a shine for that inflatable look.

### Camera
A rounded rectangle with a circle lens and a small button on top. Snap those summer memories.

### Lemonade stand
A box counter with a sign on top reading lemonade. Add a pitcher and a few cups.

### Beach bag
A wide tote shape with two handles. Let a towel and a hat poke out the top.

## Summer animals

### Dolphin
A smooth curved body with a pointed nose and a triangle fin. Draw it jumping over a wave line.

### Sea turtle
A round dome shell with patch shapes on top. Add a small head and four flipper shapes.

### Seagull
A simple body with two wing curves drawn like a wide M. Add a few in the sky for a beach feel.

### Butterfly
An oval body with two wing shapes on each side. Add little dot patterns and color it bright.

### Bumblebee
A round striped oval with two clear wings and tiny antennae. Add a buzzing motion line.

### Ladybug
A round red dome with a center line and black dots. Add a small head and tiny legs.

### Frog by a pond
A wide oval with two big eye bumps on top. Add little webbed feet and a lily pad below.

### Tropical fish
A simple oval body with a fan tail and a round eye. Color it in bright stripes.

### Goldfish in a bowl
A round bowl with a wavy water line. Add a little orange fish with a flowy tail inside.

## Tips

- Stick to sky blue, sunny yellow, coral, and mint for a true summer palette.
- Round off your shapes. Soft, bouncy edges feel warm and happy.
- Add a smiling sun or a few wave lines around any drawing to set the summer mood.
- Layer drip lines on treats and shine spots on glass for a cool, juicy look.
- Draw light first, then darken your favorite lines for a clean finish.

## FAQ

### What are easy summer drawings for beginners?
A smiling sun and an ice cream cone are the easiest place to start. The sun is just a circle with triangle rays, and a cone is a triangle with round scoops on top. Add a beach ball and a wave and your summer page fills up fast.

### What are good summer sketch ideas for a sketchbook?
Try small summer sketch ideas like seashells, popsicles, sunglasses, and starfish. These fit anywhere on a page and string together into one sunny beach scene. A rainbow across the top ties it all together.
