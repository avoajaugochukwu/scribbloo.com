Looking for fall drawing ideas and easy autumn doodles? You are in the right spot. This list is packed with autumn things to draw using simple shapes like ovals, triangles, and soft curves. No skill needed. If you can draw a leaf and a round pumpkin, you can make almost everything here. We cover colorful leaves, pumpkins and harvest, cozy fall items, autumn animals, and warm sweaters and drinks. The trick to that fall and autumn feeling is simple: warm colors and soft, rounded shapes. Grab a pencil and let's make something cozy.

## How to use this list

Pick any idea that catches your eye. You do not need to go in order. Each idea names the easy starting shape so you know exactly where to begin. Draw light at first, then darken the lines you love. When you are done, color it in with reds, oranges, golds, and browns for that classic autumn glow.

## Autumn leaves

### Maple leaf
Start with a wide star like shape and round the points. Add a center vein and side veins.

### Oak leaf
A long oval with wavy, bumpy edges. Draw a center line down the middle for the vein.

### Falling leaf
A simple leaf oval tilted sideways with a tiny stem. Add motion lines to show it drifting.

### Leaf pile
A bumpy mound of overlapping leaf shapes. Color it in mixed reds, oranges, and yellows.

### Leaf garland
A wavy string with small leaves hanging at intervals. Great across the top of a page.

### Acorn
An oval nut with a textured cap on top. Add a tiny stem and crosshatch lines on the cap.

### Twig with leaves
A curved branch line with a few leaves branching off. Add an acorn for autumn charm.

### Veiny leaf close up
One big leaf filling the space with detailed vein lines fanning out from the center.

## Pumpkins and harvest

### Round pumpkin
A wide oval with curved ridge lines down the front. Add a green stem and a leaf.

### Pumpkin stack
Three pumpkins piled smallest on top. Add stems and a few scattered leaves.

### Striped gourd
A bumpy oval with a curved neck. Color it in alternating autumn stripes.

### Corn on the cob
A long oval filled with little kernel squares. Add two leaf husks peeling back.

### Wheat bundle
Several tall stems tied in the middle with little grain shapes at the tops.

### Apple basket
A woven basket shape filled with round apples peeking over the top.

### Hay bale
A rounded rectangle with horizontal lines for the straw. Stack a pumpkin on top.

### Harvest cornucopia
A curved horn shape spilling out pumpkins, apples, and leaves from the wide end.

## Cozy fall items

### Knit scarf
A long wavy band with fringe lines at both ends. Color it in cozy stripes.

### Rain boots
Two tall boot shapes side by side. Add a leaf stuck to one and a little shine.

### Open umbrella
A half circle dome with curved ribs underneath and a J shaped handle.

### Candle in a jar
A round jar with a small flame and a glow. Add a label and a soft light.

### Plaid blanket
A folded rectangle with crisscross plaid lines. Add a fringe edge for warmth.

### Lantern
A box shape with a curved top handle and a small glow inside. Hang it from a hook.

### Stack of books
Three or four books piled up with a mug resting on top. So cozy for autumn.

### Wool socks
Two thick sock shapes with a folded cuff. Add a tiny knit pattern.

## Autumn animals

### Cozy fox
A pointy face with two ears and a big fluffy tail curled around. Add a small scarf.

### Squirrel with acorn
An oval body with a big curvy tail. Add tiny paws holding a round acorn.

### Hedgehog
A round body covered in short spike lines. Add a small pointed face and a leaf on its back.

### Owl on a branch
A puffed up oval with two big eyes and a small beak. Perch it on a branch line.

### Deer
An oval head with two branching antlers and gentle eyes. Add a small leaf nearby.

### Migrating bird
A simple body with pointed wings, drawn as a wide M. Add a few in a row across the sky.

### Chipmunk
A small oval with stripe lines down the back. Add round cheeks full of acorns.

### Bear with a scarf
A round bear body and head with little ears. Wrap a cozy scarf around its neck.

## Sweaters and drinks

### Cozy sweater
A wide shirt shape with long sleeves. Add a crew neck and a knit pattern across the front.

### Pumpkin spice latte
A tall cup with a lid and a sleeve. Add steam swirls and a little leaf design.

### Hot apple cider
A clear mug with a cinnamon stick and an apple slice. Add steam rising up.

### Warm beanie
A rounded hat shape with a folded brim and a pom on top. Color it in autumn tones.

### Tea with a teabag
A simple cup with a string and tag hanging over the rim. Add gentle steam.

### Flannel shirt
A shirt shape with a collar and plaid crisscross lines. Roll up the sleeves.

### Marshmallow cocoa
A mug topped with little marshmallow circles. Add steam and a candy stick.

### Mittens pair
Two rounded mitten shapes with a thumb on each. Connect them with a yarn string.

### Cardigan with buttons
An open jacket shape with a row of button dots down the front and two pockets.

### Thermos
A tall cylinder with a screw top cap. Add a leaf sticker and a strap loop.

These autumn things to draw all share one trick: soft, rounded shapes plus warm colors equal instant fall and autumn coziness.

## Tips

- Stick to reds, oranges, golds, and browns for a true fall and autumn palette.
- Round off your shapes. Soft edges feel warm and cozy.
- Add a few falling leaves around any drawing to set the autumn mood.
- Layer steam lines on drinks and soft glow on candles for warmth.
- Draw light first, then darken your best lines for a clean finish.

## FAQ

### What are easy fall drawing ideas for beginners?
Leaves and round pumpkins are the easiest place to start. A maple leaf is just a rounded star, and a pumpkin is an oval with ridge lines. Add acorns and a cozy mug and your autumn page fills up fast.

### What are good autumn doodles for a sketchbook?
Try small autumn doodles like acorns, scarves, lanterns, and steaming mugs. These fit anywhere on a page and string together into a cozy fall scene. A leaf garland across the top ties everything together.
